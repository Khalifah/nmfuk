Orphans Sponsorship Scheme

A cause very dear to us!

Orphan sponsorship is one of the key projects Nigeria Muslim Forum UK undertake. It is highly recommended in Islam to look after the needy especially orphans, as Prophet Muhammad peace be upon him has clearly stated that those who look after orphans will be raised side by side with him on the day of judgment.

The programme is run in conjunction with partners in the host community. These are charitable organisations who are able to monitor judicious use of funds to this orphans, their welfare and performance in schools. All requests for Orphan Sponsorship are subjected to vigorous appraisals before any approval. We call on all our brothers and sisters to consider sponsoring orphans as this is one of the highest virtues of faith. Suggestions were made as to how NMF UK can secure sponsorship for its orphans.

About us

The Nigeria Muslim Forum (NMFUK) is a registered charity organisation under the Charity Commission of England and Wales with registration number 1144040. The forum is managed voluntarily by Nigerian Muslim professionals and students in the UK and Nigeria.

NMFUK is an affiliate of Council of Nigerian Muslim Organizations (CNMO), which in turn is a member of the coordinating council of the Muslim Council of Britain (MCB).

OUR MISSION

  • Support and serve our communities and help them towards fulfilling their spiritual needs
  • Promote education and health interventions to marginalised communities in developing countries to prepare them for meaningful participation in the society
  • Support and respond to communities during time of needs such as during emergencies
